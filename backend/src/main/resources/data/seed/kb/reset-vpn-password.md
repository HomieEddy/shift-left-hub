# How to Reset Your VPN Password

If you have forgotten your VPN password or need to reset it for security reasons, follow the steps below.

## Prerequisites
- Access to your registered email address
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)

## Step 1: Visit the Self-Service Portal
Open your browser and navigate to **https://portal.company.com/vpn-reset**.

## Step 2: Enter Your Email Address
Enter the email address associated with your VPN account and click **Send Reset Link**.

## Step 3: Check Your Email
You will receive an email with a password reset link. This link expires within **30 minutes**.

## Step 4: Create a New Password
Click the link and create a new password. Your password must:
- Be at least **12 characters** long
- Contain at least **one uppercase letter**
- Contain at least **one number**
- Contain at least **one special character** (`!@#$%^&*`)

## Step 5: Confirm and Log In
Confirm your new password and click **Reset**. You can now log in to the VPN client with your new password.

## Need Help?
Contact the IT Service Desk at **ext. 4357** or email **servicedesk@company.com**.
