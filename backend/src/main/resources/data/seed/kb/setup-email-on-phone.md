# Setting Up Company Email on Your Mobile Device

You can access your company email from your smartphone using either the **Microsoft Outlook app** or your device's built-in **Mail** app.

## Recommended: Microsoft Outlook App

### iOS
1. Install **Microsoft Outlook** from the App Store
2. Open the app and tap **Get Started**
3. Enter your full email address (`firstname.lastname@company.com`)
4. Tap **Add Account**
5. Enter your network password
6. Accept the permissions (required for calendar sync)
7. Your mail, calendar, and contacts will sync automatically

### Android
1. Install **Microsoft Outlook** from Google Play Store
2. Open the app and tap **Get Started**
3. Enter your full email address
4. Enter your network password
5. Enable **Sync Calendar** and **Sync Contacts** if prompted
6. Your mailbox is ready

## Alternative: Device Mail App

### iOS (Apple Mail)
1. Go to **Settings > Mail > Accounts > Add Account > Microsoft Exchange**
2. Enter your full email address and password
3. Tap **Next** — settings will be auto-configured
4. Toggle **Mail**, **Contacts**, and **Calendars** as desired
5. Tap **Save**

### Android (Gmail / Samsung Email)
1. Open the **Email** or **Gmail** app
2. Tap **Add Account > Exchange**
3. Enter your full email address and password
4. Enter **outlook.office365.com** as the server address (if prompted)
5. Accept the security policies
6. Your email is now configured

## Troubleshooting
- **"Password incorrect"**: Forgot your password? Reset it at [portal.company.com/password-reset](https://portal.company.com/password-reset)
- **"Server not found"**: Check your mobile data or Wi-Fi connection
- **Two-factor authentication**: You may need to create an **app password** from the company portal

## Security
- Enable **device PIN or biometric lock** on your phone
- If your device is lost or stolen, contact IT immediately to remote-wipe company data
- Do not forward company email to personal accounts
