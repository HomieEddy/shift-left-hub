# Office Locations & Facilities

## Headquarters — Montreal

**Address:**
1250 Boulevard René-Lévesque Ouest
Montreal, QC H3B 4W8
Canada

**Building Access:**
- Office hours: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM (Monday - Friday)
- Access after hours requires badge access (enabled on request)
- Visitors must check in at the lobby security desk (bring photo ID)

**Parking:**
- Underground parking available (monthly pass or hourly rate)
- Bicycle storage room on level P1
- Nearest metro: Lucien-L'Allier (orange line)

**Floor Plan:**
| Floor | Departments |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Lobby, Security, HR Suite, Cafeteria |
| 2 | IT Support Center, Legal, Facilities |
| 3 | Executive, Engineering, Meeting Rooms |
| 4 | Operations, Client Services |

## Regional Office — Toronto

**Address:**
100 King Street West, Suite 2400
Toronto, ON M5X 1C9
Canada

**Building Access:**
- Office hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Monday - Friday)
- Key fob access required for entry
- Visitors: register at the concierge desk on the main floor

## Regional Office — Vancouver

**Address:**
401 West Georgia Street, Suite 1500
Vancouver, BC V6B 5A1
Canada

**Building Access:**
- Office hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Monday - Friday)
- Badge access required after 6:00 PM
- Visitor parking available in the building garage

## Regional Office — New York

**Address:**
350 Fifth Avenue, Suite 3200
New York, NY 10118
United States

**Building Access:**
- Office hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Monday - Friday)
- Security check-in required at lobby
- Valid government ID required for entry

## Amenities

All offices provide the following amenities:

- Fully stocked kitchenettes (coffee, tea, snacks)
- Stand-up desks (adjustable)
- Meeting room booking via Outlook
- Secure bike storage
- Showers and changing rooms
- Mother's rooms
- Quiet rooms for focused work

# Emplacements des bureaux et installations

## Siège social — Montréal

**Adresse :**
1250 Boulevard René-Lévesque Ouest
Montréal, QC H3B 4W8
Canada

**Accès au bâtiment :**
- Heures de bureau : 7 h - 19 h (lundi - vendredi)
- L'accès après les heures nécessite un badge (activé sur demande)
- Les visiteurs doivent s'enregistrer au bureau de sécurité du hall (pièce d'identité avec photo requise)

**Stationnement :**
- Stationnement souterrain disponible (abonnement mensuel ou tarif horaire)

- Métro le plus proche : Lucien-L'Allier (ligne orange)

**Plan d'étage :**
