## Emergency Contacts

| Situation | Contact |
|-----------|---------|

## After-Hours Support

For critical IT issues outside business hours:

1. Call extension 4500
2. You will be connected to the on-call technician
3. Provide your name, department, and a description of the issue
4. The technician will respond within 30 minutes for critical issues
