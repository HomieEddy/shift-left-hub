# Company Password Policy and Account Security

Protecting company data starts with strong account security. This article outlines the password requirements and security practices every employee must follow.

## Password Requirements

All company passwords must comply with the following rules:

| Requirement | Standard |
|---|---|
| Minimum length | **12 characters** |
| Complexity | At least 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special character |
| Password history | Cannot reuse last **10 passwords** |
| Maximum age | **90 days** — you will be prompted to change |
| Account lockout | After **5 failed attempts** (30-minute lockout) |

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA is **mandatory** for all accounts. Here is how to set it up:

### Step 1: Install an Authenticator App
- **iOS/Android:** Microsoft Authenticator, Google Authenticator, or Authy

### Step 2: Register Your Device
1. Go to [portal.company.com/security/mfa](https://portal.company.com/security/mfa)
2. Click **Set Up MFA**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm

### Step 3: Backup Codes
Save your **one-time backup codes** in a secure location (not on your computer). These can be used if you lose your phone.

## Account Security Best Practices

- **Never share your password** with anyone, including IT staff
- IT will **never ask** for your password via email or phone
- **Lock your computer** when stepping away (`Win + L` on Windows, `Ctrl + Cmd + Q` on macOS)
- Use a **password manager** (Bitwarden, 1Password, or KeePass) — IT recommends Bitwarden
- **Log out** of company systems when finished, especially on shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## What to Do If You Suspect a Breach

1. **Change your password immediately** at [portal.company.com/password-reset](https://portal.company.com/password-reset)
2. **Contact IT Security** at **security@company.com** or **ext. 4388**
3. If you lose your phone, deactivate MFA at [portal.company.com/security/mfa](https://portal.company.com/security/mfa)
